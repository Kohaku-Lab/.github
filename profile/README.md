## 🧪 Who We Are

**Kohaku Lab** is a virtual-first research lab — no office, no corporate structure, just people who love building AI/ML tools and doing research together across time zones.

We all use text-to-image models, so we trained one at home (**HDM**). We all used HuggingFace and got frustrated with policy shifts, so we built **KohakuHub**. Tired of W&B limitations? We made **KohakuBoard**. The philosophy is simple: **scratch your own itch**.

Led by **[KohakuBlueLeaf](https://github.com/KohakuBlueleaf)** (Shih-Ying Yeh) and joined by contributors worldwide, we balance research and practical tooling — proving that you don't need a big company to do interesting AI work.

## 🔬 Research Highlights

- **PEFT / Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning** — LyCORIS pushes the boundaries of LoRA-family methods
- **Generative Models** — TIPO, Kohaku-XL, HDM: practical text-to-image research
- **MLOps Tooling** — Self-hosted experiment tracking & model management for real workflows
- **Open Access Philosophy** — Every model, every tool, every dataset — freely available
